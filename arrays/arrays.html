Arrays

Problem

Write a program that asks the user to type 10 integers into an array. The program must compute and print out how many integers are greater than or equal to 10.

Example

jharvard@run.cs50.net (~): ./arrays
5 
22 
18 
4 
8 
11 
19 
22 
100 
1 

  Output: 6

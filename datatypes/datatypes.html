Playing with Data Types

Problem

Declare and one int variable, x, and one float variable, y, and set their values to arbitrary int and float values, respectively. Then declare two new variables -- also an int and a float -- and store the sum of x and y in both of them. Print both sums. What do you attribute the difference to? Place your answer to this question in a comment at the end of your program.
